Where It All Started

Hello! My name is Taylor Shell. From the age of three I was quite active. I played t-ball, soccer, gymanstics, etc. Nothing quite struck a cord with me like soccer. Soccer was my everything and I didn't want to do anything else. From that moment, I have stuck with it since.

First Love

Soccer was the only sport I fell in love with. I would always have a ball at my feet 24/7. You couldnt keep away from the game. I was the smallest out there but I was definetley the toughest. Only at about three feet tall I was blazing through other people. As I got older I tried out for a more competitive team.

Finding My Feet
