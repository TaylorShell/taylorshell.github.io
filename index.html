Taylor Shell: Background Information

My name is Taylor Shell. I was born in Manhattan, New York but was raised in Richmond, Virginia. Growing up I was one of the most active kids. I tried every sport from t-ball, to dance, to basketball, and to soccer. Soccer was the sport that I fell in love with first. At the age of three my mom signed me up on the YMCA youth soccer team. I was the smallest one on my team. My shingurads looked like cinderblocks around my calves and my jersey was so long it could touch my toes. That didn't stop me from being one of the most energetic people on the field.

At the age of eight I made my first travel team. Once I joined I knew I wanted to play soccer forever. Around the age of 13, I knew I could see a future at the collegiate level. After months of working my tail off I was given the opportunity to attend the University of Texas at the age of 14.

At 16, I made the biggest decision on my life and committed to continue my athletic and academic career at the University of Miami. All the missed school dances, private lessons, and going out to work on my craft when everyone was taking a break was well worth it. I know get to live out my dream and I would never change a thing.
