Introduction

Denmark is in Northern Europe. The capital of Denmark is Copenhagen. The countries that border Denmark are the Netherlands, Germany, and Poland. Denmark was one of the most visited countries by Vikings.

Terrain

Denmark's terrains from the naked eye looks flat but it's territory is thousands of acres of rolling plains that are flat and low.

Climate

Denmark in the summer is often cool and humid. In the winter Denmark is frigid and has heavy winds.
