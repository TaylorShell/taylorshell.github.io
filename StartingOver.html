Who is Taylor Shell...

The Injury

My sophmore year of highschool would be one of the hardest years of my life. I waw used to facing adversity but think was a different animal. On April 2nd I tore my ACL. Otherwise known as your Anterior Cruciate Ligament. I was in shock when I heard the results. My first initial thought was my soccer career was over. From that moment I was reliving the acitons leading up to the event. Whether I could have done something different. I was told I can only control what I can control. I looked at the Injury as a time reset the clock and work on myself.

Rehab

After realizing my soccer career was coming to a pause I knew I had to focus on getting back to the person I was. The catch was getting stronger because my injury resulted in a area I was lacking. Early mornings at 6 A.M. I would go to my first session than come back in the afternoon to continue my rehab. Grinding day in and day out to get back playing the game I love. After a long eight months of gruiling rehab I was able to get back on the field.

First Day Back

My first day back on the field I was nervous but it was a good nervous. I waited so long to get back on the field. My first touch on the ball I assisted my teammate for her first ever goal. My second touch of the ball I scored a goal. I was ambushed by my teammates. I was overwhelmed with joy because I knew I was back. I was at a lost of words. After the game I was so close to breaking down in tears but I was rushed my teammates with hugs and happy cries.
