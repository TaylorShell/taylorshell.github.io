The Injury

My sophmore year of highschool would be one of the hardest years of my life. I waw used to facing adversity but think was a different animal. On April 2nd I tore my ACL. Otherwise known as your Anterior Cruciate Ligament. I was in shock when I heard the results. My first initial thought was my soccer career was over. From that moment I was reliving the acitons leading up to the event. Whether I could have done something different. I was told I can only control what I can control. I looked at the Injury as a time reset the clock and work on myself.
